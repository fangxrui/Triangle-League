About Us 关于我们

Triangle League is a multi-tiered sports league for the Asian community based in Research Triangle Park, North Carolina. It provides high quality event services for many different types of sports, including professional venues and referees, multi-angle video recording, comprehensive technical statistics, etc. Triangle League aims to help more people participate in sports while enjoying the competition and learning the sportsmanship.

Triangle League是一个面向亚洲社区的多层次体育联盟，总部设在北卡罗来纳州的三角研究公园。该联赛在众多不同类型的体育项目上都提供高品质的赛事服务，包括专业场地和裁判，多角度视频记录，全面的技术统计等。Triangle League旨在帮助更多的人参与体育运动，同时享受比赛，学习体育精神。

Triangle League

联赛成立

Triangle League began in 2023 in a Tennessee bar during March Madness. We are very fond of American sports culture and regret that Asians cannot participate in high-level sports events in the United States. Therefore, the main purpose of Triangle League is to create a platform for Asians who love sports and want to compete at a high level, and to establish a friendly and open sportsmanship environment.

Triangle League始于2023年疯狂三月期间田纳西州的一家酒吧。我们非常喜欢美国的体育文化，并且很遗憾亚洲人无法在美国参与到高水平的体育赛事。因此，Triangle League的主要目的是为喜欢运动并希望参加高水平比赛的亚洲人创造一个平台，并建立一个友好和开放的体育精神环境。
